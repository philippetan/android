Account Deletion Request

At Pawfectly Adoptable, we respect your privacy and provide options for you to control your personal information. If you wish to delete your account, please follow the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account within the Pawfectly Adoptable app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Pawfectly Adoptable app and go to Settings.
  2. Select Delete Account.
  3. Enter your password to confirm the account deletion.

Data Types Deleted

Upon confirming account deletion, the following personal information will be permanently removed from our records:

  • Name
  • Contact Details (email, phone number, address)
  • Photos of valid IDs and certificates
  • Profile information and adoption history
  • Message history with shelters and other users
